Operator Precedence and Associativity

Operator Precedence

Operator precedence determines which operator is evaluated first in an expression. Higher precedence operators are evaluated before lower precedence operators. 

result = 2 + 3 * 4
print(result)

Multiplication has higher precedence than addition, so 3 * 4 is evaluated first.

Operator Associativity

Associativity defines the order of evaluation when operators have the same precedence. 

value = 10 - 5 - 2
print(value)

Subtraction follows left-to-right associativity: (10 - 5) - 2
